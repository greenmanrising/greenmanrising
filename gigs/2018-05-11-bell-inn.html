Friday May 11th 2018

Greenman Rising “acoustic”, at The Bell Inn.

We’re coming back to the Bell Inn, so if you’re interested, get those tickets booked as this sold out last year.

No PA system, no electric instruments, microphones and pickups - just us, our instruments and a select audience.

The Bell Inn is a smaller venue, and we’re expecting this to be a popular night again, so get in quick for tickets!

Tickets: Â£10
Starts: 8pm

Telephone the Bell Inn to reserve tickets: 01295 810338

You can also drop in at the Bell Inn to buy tickets.

The Bell Inn itself is a fantastic venue. We discovered it when recording our CD in nearby Bodicote; it was our midday sanctuary for drink and sustenance away from the studio.

They won Hook Norton Brewery’s Pub of the Year 2016, Community Pub of the Year 2016 and the Beer Quality & Cellar Management Pub of the Year 2016 awards. Hook Norton were themselves announced as The Good Pub Guide’s Brewery of the Year 2017.

The Bell Inn also play host to some top folk bands such as Belshazzers Feast, Pilgrim’s Way and Will Pound and Eddy Jay, so they are well worth keeping an eye on for good gigs.
